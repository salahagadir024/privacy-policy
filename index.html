# Privacy Policy for Origami Studio **Effective date:** March 25, 2026 **Last updated:** March 25, 2026 Origami Studio ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application. ## 1. Scope This Privacy Policy applies to the Origami Studio iOS application and related support communications. ## 2. Information We Collect ### 2.1 Information You Provide We may receive information you voluntarily provide when contacting support (for example, your email address and the content of your message). ### 2.2 Information Stored on Your Device To provide core app functionality, the app stores certain data locally on your device, such as: - Favorite origami models - App preferences and settings This local data is not transmitted to our servers by default. ### 2.3 Automatically Collected Data At this time, Origami Studio is designed to operate without mandatory account creation and without collecting personal data for profiling or advertising purposes. If we enable analytics or crash reporting in future versions, this policy will be updated to describe: - What is collected - Why it is collected - Which providers are used ## 3. How We Use Information We use information only to: - Provide and maintain app functionality - Respond to support requests - Improve reliability, performance, and user experience We do **not** sell personal information. ## 4. Legal Bases (Where Applicable) If you are in a region that requires legal bases for processing (e.g., GDPR), we rely on: - **Performance of a contract** (to provide app features you request) - **Legitimate interests** (to maintain and improve app quality) - **Consent** (where required, e.g., optional analytics) ## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure We do not share personal data with third parties except: - When required by law, legal process, or governmental request - To protect rights, safety, and security - With service providers strictly necessary to operate support or technical infrastructure (if applicable), under appropriate safeguards ## 6. Data Retention - Local app data remains on your device until removed by you (e.g., uninstalling the app or clearing preferences). - Support emails or inquiries may be retained as needed to resolve issues and comply with legal obligations. ## 7. International Transfers If we use service providers in other countries in the future, we will implement appropriate safeguards as required by applicable law. ## 8. Your Privacy Rights Depending on your location, you may have rights to: - Access personal data - Correct inaccurate data - Request deletion - Restrict or object to processing - Data portability - Withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent) To exercise rights, contact us at the email below. ## 9.
